Land and farm service: sign in

Land and farm service is new. We are developing the service to:

  • replace the Rural Payments service
  • provide farmers and land owners with a single account where they can access a range of services to manage their land

What you can use this service for now

You can use the service if you:

  • want to apply for and claim funding to improve animal health and welfare
  • need to update your business and personal details because they are incorrect

What you'll be able to use this service for in future

In future more people will be able to use the service to manage a range of tasks related to their land or farm. This includes:

  • applying for or managing schemes such as Countryside Stewardship and Sustainable Farming Incentive
  • managing who has access permission to act on behalf of your business
  • viewing payment details
  • finding new grants and funding opportunities
  • managing your land and maps
