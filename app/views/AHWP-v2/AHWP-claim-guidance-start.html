Guidance

Claim funding to improve animal health and welfare

Find out how to claim for animal health and welfare reviews and endemic disease follow-ups.

From: Department for Environment, Food & Rural Affairs and Rural Payments Agency
Published 19 June 2024
Last updated 26 June 2025 - See all updates

Applies to England

This service forms part of the Animal Health and Welfare Pathway. It will provide payments to livestock keepers to fund farm visits by vets.

Start a claim for funding

You can claim funding if you applied for and set up a 'Get funding to improve animal health and welfare' (IAHW) agreement before the vet visit.

You’ll need:

  • your Rural Payments service customer reference number (CRN) and password to sign in
  • the summary the vet gave you
Start now

Before you start

Your IAHW claim may be delayed if your details are not correct. If your information has recently changed you should check and updated the details we hold for you. The fastest way to do this is to use the new Land and farm service.

Update your details on the Land and farm service.

What information you'll need to provide

The vet will give you a summary of the animal health and welfare review or endemic disease follow-up, depending on what you applied for.

When you claim you’ll need to give details from the vet’s summary. Find out what must be included in a:

  • cattle review summary
  • pig review summary
  • sheep review summary
  • cattle follow-up summary
  • pig follow-up summary
  • sheep follow-up summary

You need to have completed a biosecurity assessment if you are claiming for a follow-up for:

  • cattle
  • pigs

When to make a claim

If you are claiming for both an animal health and welfare review and endemic disease follow-up, your review claim must be approved before you can claim for the resulting follow-up. You must have followed the guidance on planning reviews and planning follow-ups.

The service ends on 19 June 2027. Your review and follow-up visits must be done by then. You must subnit your claims by 19 September 2027.

Get help with your claim

For more help with claiming, you can watch the videos that show you how to make a claim for:

  • sheep review
  • beef cattle review
  • dairy cattle review
  • pig review
  • sheep follow-up
  • pig follow-up
  • cattle follow-up

Contact the Rural Payments Agency and give your IAHW agreement number if you:

  • cannot claim online
  • have a question about your claim
