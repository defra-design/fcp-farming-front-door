Update your details

If your details are not correct you need to update them before you can get funding to improve animal health and welfare (IAHW). The fastest way to do this is to use the new Land and farm service.

Update your details on the Land and farm service.

What the Land and farm service is

We are developing the Land and farm service to:

  • replace the Rural Payments service
  • provide farmers and land owners witha single account where they can access a range of services to manage their land

You can use the Land and farm service if you:

  • want to apply for or claim funding to improve animal health and welfare
  • need to update your business and personal details because the are incorrect

In future more people will be able to use the service to manage a range of tasks related to their land or farm. This includes:

  • applying for or managing schemes such as Countryside Stewardship and Sustainable Farming Incentive
  • managing who has permission to act on behalf of your business
  • viewing payment details
  • finding new grants and funding opportunities
  • managing your land and maps
