What is Hugo Smith’s relationship with Agile Farm Ltd?

Why do we need to know the business relationship?

We need to know this so we can identify the relationships of the people who are involved in this business, to protect against potential fraud.

Someone's relationship could affect what they are normally allowed to do for the business if there is a conflict of interest. For example, a vet is not allowed to submit a claim as a business owner for a vet visit they provided evidence for.
