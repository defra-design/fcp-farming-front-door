Farm payments technical test (FPTT): Confirmation of your participation and next steps

Thank you for agreeing to take part in the Farm payments technical test (FPTT).

Before you can take part in the FPTT, you need to:

  • check your maps are up to date
  • choose a date for a prospective RPA visit
  • check if you can access the digital service
  • check your details are correct
  • get an SFI historic Environment Farm Environment Record (SFI HEFER) if you need one
  • complete the consent form
  • read the FPTT information and the terms and conditions
  • inform us if there are any issues

Check your maps are up to date

We have attached a copy of your maps to this email. These maps are taken from the data we hold for you on the Rural Payments service.

Check the information on these maps is correct. You need to check the parcel number and hectarage for each parcel.

If you make any changes to your land parcels, such as merging or selling parcels, in the time between receiving this email and submitting your FPTT application, please let us know by:

  • calling us on 020 8026 2395
  • emailing us at farmpayments@rpa.gov.uk

Choose a date for a prospective RPA visit

You will be applying for the FPTT using our new digital service. We’d like to visit you when you’re completing your application, to give advice and find out more about your experience of applying.

If you would be interested in a visit, please reply to this email, or call us on 020 8026 395, to tell us any dates you’re available. We’ll let you know as soon as possible if we’re able to visit you.

If you book a visit and later decide you no longer want to take part in the FPTT, please let us know before the date of the visit.

Check if you can access the digital service

If you book an application visit, please check you can open the sign-in page for the digital service before your visit. When we visit, you will be able to log in and begin your application.

When you click the digital service link, your browser should open a webpage which asks you to sign into the farm and land service by entering your customer reference number (CRN) and Rural Payments service password.

Please don’t attempt to log into the service until your application visit, or until we advise you to.

Get an SFI Historic Environment Farm Environment Record (SFI HEFER)

If you apply for FPTT actions on land with historic or archaelogical features, you must request an SFI HEFER from Historic England before we can offer you an agreement.

Complete the consent form

As the FPTT is a test of a new digital service, your participation is considered research. Before you take part, we need to check you understand what’s involved and record your consent using an online form.

You must complete the consent form before the application visit.

Read the FPTT information and the terms and conditions

We have attached the FPTT information, actions and terms and conditions to this email. You must read these documents before the application visit.

They cover:

  • when and how much you’ll get paid
  • eligibility for the FPTT
  • how the FPTT works with funding schemes you might already be a part of
  • the regulatory consents, licenses and permissions you’ll need for the FPTT
  • how to manage your FPTT agreement
  • how to do the FPTT actions

Inform us if there are any issues

When you have completed the above tasks, please get in touch with us if:

  • your map information is incorrect, or you have made any changes to your maps
  • your maps you are unable to access the digital service
  • your details are incorrect and and you cannot update them
  • you are unable to complete the consent form
  • you have any further questions about this email
  • you no longer wish to take part in the FPTT

You can contact us by replying to this email, or by calling us on 020 8026 2395.

Please include your SBI, which you can find at the top of this email, on anything you send us.
