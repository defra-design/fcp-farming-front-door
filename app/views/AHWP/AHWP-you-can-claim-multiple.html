You can claim for multiple species as part of this agreement

Under this agreement, you can claim funding for the following species:

  • beef cattle
  • dairy cattle
  • pigs
  • sheep

You can do a maximum of 3 reviews and 3 follow-ups per species. You must do all your reviews and follow-ups by 19 June 2027.

If you did a review in the old annual health and welfare service, it will not count towards your limit.

You need to make a separate claim for each species.
