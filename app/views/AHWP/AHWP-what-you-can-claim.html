What you can claim for as part of this agreement

Under this agreement, you can claim funding for any number of herds or flocks of one or more of the following species:

  • beef cattle
  • dairy cattle
  • pigs
  • sheep

You can do a maximum of 3 reviews and 3 follow-ups on any number of herds or flocks of one or more species. You must do all your reviews and follow-ups by 19 June 2027.

You need to make a separate claim for each herd or flock of a particular species
