We are developing the Land and farm service to replace the Rural Payments service. It will provide a simple way for farmers and land owners to access a range of services from a single account.

What you can use this service for now

You can use the Land and farm service to update your personal or business details if you are applying or claiming funding to improve animal health and welfare (IAHW).

Only people who are applying or claiming funding for an IAHW agreement can currently use the service.

What you'll be able to use this service for in future

In future more people will be able to use the service to manage a range of tasks related to their land or farm. This includes:

  • applying for or managing schemes such as Countryside Stewardship and Sustainable Farming Incentive
  • managing who has permission to act on behalf of your business
  • viewing payment details
  • finding new grants and funding opportunities
  • managing your land and maps
