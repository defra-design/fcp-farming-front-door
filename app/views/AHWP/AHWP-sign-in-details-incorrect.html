Update your details

If your details are not correct you need to update them before you can get funding to improve animal health and welfare (IAHW). You'll need to use the Land and farm service to do this.

Update your details on the Land and farm service.

What the Land and farm service is

We are developing the Land and farm service to replace the Rural Payments service. It will provide a simple way for farmers and land owners to access a range of services from a single account.

Only people who are applying or claiming funding under an IAHW agreement can currently use the service.

In future people will be able to use the service to manage a range of tasks related to their land or farm. This includes:

  • applying for or managing schemes such as Countryside Stewardship and Sustainable Farming Incentive
  • managing who has permission to act on behalf of your business
  • viewing payment details
  • finding new grants and funding opportunities
  • managing your land and maps
