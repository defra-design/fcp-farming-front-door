Update your details

Your application to Improve Animal Health and Welfare (IAHW) may be delayed if your details are not correct. You should update your details now. The fastest way to do this is to use the new Land and farm service.

Update your details on the Land and farm service.

What the Land and farm service is

We are developing the Land and farm service to:

  • replace the Rural Payments service
  • provide farmers and land owners witha single account where they can access a range of services to manage their land

You can use the Land and farm service if:

  • you want to apply for or claim funding to improve animal health and welfare (IAHW)
  • the business and personal details we hold about you are incorrect

In future more people will be able to use the service to manage a range of tasks related to their land or farm. Some of the tasks you can already do on the Rural Payments service. Other tasks will be made available to people for the first time.

Eventually you will be able to:

  • apply for or manage schemes such as Countryside Stewardship and Sustainable Farming Incentive
  • manage who has permission to act on behalf of your business
  • view payment details
  • find new grants and funding opportunities
  • manage your land and maps
