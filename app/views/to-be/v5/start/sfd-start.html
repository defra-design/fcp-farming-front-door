We are developing a new service to replace the Rural Payments service.

'Manage your land and farm businesses' will provide a simple way to access a range of Defra services from a single account.

What you can use this service for now

You can use the service to:

  • apply and claim for funding to improve animal health and welfare
  • apply for a Calf Housing for Health and Welfare grant
  • manage your account including personal and business details and who has permission to act on behalf of your business
  • view payment details

What you'll be able to use this service for in the future

In future you'll be able to access more land and farm business tasks. This may include:

  • applying for or managing schemes such as Countryside Stewardship or Sustainable Farming Incentive
  • finding other funding opportunities
  • managing your land and maps

Read more about plans for this service.

Before you start

You'll need a Rural Payments service (RPS) account to sign into this service.
