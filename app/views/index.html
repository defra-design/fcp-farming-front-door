Buffalo Minimum Viable Product (MVP)

We've created this prototype to document what our first release of Buffalo will look like.

This prototype:

  • reflects the to-be design for the first release
  • acts as a single source of truth
  • helps ensure alignment across design, development and delivery

We will continue to use the numbered versions below to explore design options and test our ideas with users.
